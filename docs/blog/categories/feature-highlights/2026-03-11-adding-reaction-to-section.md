## Overview

Any section in an element or segment can host a **Reaction** field. Users can drag an existing reaction from their reaction list and drop it directly into the section. The reaction diagram is then rendered inline, providing immediate visual context alongside the rest of the data.

[![Overview](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/oveview.png){: .mx-auto .d-block .mb-4 .img-fluid .w-75 }](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/oveview.png){:target="_blank"}

Key benefits:

- **Contextual chemistry** – Keep reaction diagrams next to the data that references them.
- **No copy-paste** – A single drag-and-drop links the reaction; it stays in sync.
- **Works everywhere** – Available in Generic Elements and Generic Segments alike.

---

## How to Add a Reaction

Follow these steps to embed a reaction in a section:

1. **Initiate the addition** – Navigate to the desired section (e.g., *Optional Equipment*) and click the flask icon labeled **"Add reaction"**.

   [![Step 1 – Add reaction button](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/1-add-reaction-button.png){: .mx-auto .d-block .mb-4 .img-fluid .w-75 }](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/1-add-reaction-button.png){:target="_blank"}

2. **Prepare the drop area** – A dashed box appears within the section displaying **"Drop Reaction 🧪 Here"**, ready to receive your reaction.

   [![Step 2 – Drop reaction area](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/2-drop-reaction-area.png){: .mx-auto .d-block .mb-4 .img-fluid .w-75 }](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/2-drop-reaction-area.png){:target="_blank"}

3. **Select a reaction** – Open the **Reactions** list located in the panel on the left side of the screen.

   [![Step 3 – Open the reactions list](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/3-drag-a-reaction.png){: .mx-auto .d-block .mb-4 .img-fluid .w-75 }](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/3-drag-a-reaction.png){:target="_blank"}

4. **Drag and drop** – Choose the reaction you want to add, drag it from the list, and drop it directly into the dashed *"Drop Reaction Here"* area.

   [![Step 4 – Drop reaction into the area](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/4-drop-to-area.png){: .mx-auto .d-block .mb-4 .img-fluid .w-75 }](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/4-drop-to-area.png){:target="_blank"}

5. **Confirm the result** – The reaction and its corresponding chemical diagram will be displayed and embedded within the section.

   [![Step 5 – Reaction embedded in the section](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/5-final-result.png){: .mx-auto .d-block .mb-4 .img-fluid .w-75 }](/assets/images/posts/2026-03-11-adding-reaction-to-section/5-final-result.png){:target="_blank"}

## Notes & Tips

- A single Reaction field can hold **one reaction** at a time. To associate multiple reactions, add multiple Reaction fields to the layer.
- Reaction fields are fully supported in both **Generic Elements** and **Generic Segments**.
